# Privacy Policy – Heavy Fuel

Effective Date: 16 Jan 2025

Heavy Fuel is a professional aviation utility application designed for pilots.

## Data Collection
Heavy Fuel does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal user data.

## Analytics and Diagnostics
The app may use Apple’s built-in diagnostic services to collect anonymous crash and performance data. This data is not identifiable and is used solely to improve app stability.

## Third-Party Services
Heavy Fuel does not use third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services.

## User Accounts
The app does not require account creation or sign-in.

## Data Sharing
No user data is shared with third parties.
